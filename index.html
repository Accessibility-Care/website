WCAG 2.2 Compliance

Our audits are meticulously aligned with the latest Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2, ensuring your website meets international accessibility standards. We focus on:

  • visibility Perceivable: Information and UI components must be presentable to users in ways they can perceive.
  • gamepad Operable: UI components and navigation must be operable.
  • menu_book Understandable: Information and the operation of user interface must be understandable.
  • settings_applications Robust: Content must be robust enough that it can be interpreted reliably by a wide variety of user agents, including assistive technologies.
